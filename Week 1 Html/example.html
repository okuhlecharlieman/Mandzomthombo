Emptied Frames

This week marks the anniversaries of two of the most infamous art heists in history. Twenty years ago today, one version of Edvard Munch’s The Scream (he created four) was pilfered from the Munch Museum in Oslo. And on August 21, 1911, Leonardo da Vinci’s Mona Lisa was stolen from the Louvre Museum, causing an international sensation.
